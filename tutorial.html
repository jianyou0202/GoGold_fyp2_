Getting Started

Most features work without login. Login is needed for posting ideas, following, and wishlist.
1) Navigation (Desktop / Phone)
Desktop Use the top navigation bar to switch pages.
Phone Tap Menu to open or close the navigation list.
2) Register / Login
Register Create an account (stored in your browser storage).
Login Login to post ideas, comment, follow investors, and save wishlist strategies.
Logout Use the Logout link in the navigation area.
3) Dark / Light Mode
Toggle Use the Dark/Light button in the navigation. Your selection is remembered.

Home

The Home page is a dashboard: chart tools + basic trading simulator + previews of other pages.
1) Live Price
What it shows Current price and change for the selected symbol (default GC=F).
2) Chart Timeframes
Intraday 1m / 5m / 15m / 1H / 4H
Higher timeframes 1D / 1W / 1M
3) Overlays and Tools
VolumeOff by default. Toggle it on when needed.
SMA 20 / EMA 50Trend overlays to help confirm direction.
ResetResets chart zoom/pan.
FullscreenExpands the chart for analysis.
PNGExports the chart screenshot.
4) Drawing
Draw LineClick once to set start, move mouse to preview, click again to place.
Shift SnapHold Shift after the first click to snap the line to horizontal/vertical direction.
H LineOne click places a horizontal price line at the clicked level.
V LineOne click places a vertical time line at the clicked candle/time.
ClearRemoves all drawn lines.
ExitPress Esc to stop drawing mode.
5) Price Alerts
Set alertEnter a price and click Set to arm the alert.
Clear alertClick Clear to remove the alert.
6) Buy / Sell (Simulator)
PurposeA simulated account that updates your Portfolio page.
How it worksEnter oz amount and click Buy or Sell. Transactions are recorded locally.

Strategy Tester

Build strategies with indicators and parameters, then evaluate performance, drawdown, and robustness.
1) Select Indicators (Confluence)
Multiple indicatorsYou can select many indicators at once.
Confluence ruleTrades happen only when ALL selected indicators agree on BUY or SELL.
2) Parameters + Tooltips
Dynamic paramsParameter inputs appear based on selected indicators.
DefinitionsHover indicator names and result labels to see definitions.
3) Risk & Costs
Commission + slippageMakes results realistic by deducting costs.
SL/TPStops and targets are evaluated using candle high/low.
Net ProfitFinal equity minus starting equity after ALL costs.
4) Run Backtest + Outputs
Backtest ResultsMetrics like Sharpe, drawdown, win rate, and net profit.
Equity & DrawdownCharts to visualize growth and risk.
Trade logEntry/exit rows and the BUY/EXIT markers shown on the backtest chart.
5) Walk-Forward Testing
WhyChecks whether performance holds across multiple time segments.
HowEnable Walk-Forward, choose folds/start/test percentages, then run backtest.
6) Robustness & Stress
Robustness scoreCombines risk/return and stability measures to flag fragile strategies.
Monte CarloResamples trade outcomes to estimate risk of bad sequences.
7) Presets + Wishlist + Demo
Save PresetStore your strategy settings for later.
Add to WishlistBookmark strategies to revisit.
Demo RunOne-click example strategy for quick demonstrations.

Portfolio

A simulated trading ledger with deposits/withdrawals, holdings valuation, and equity curve.
1) Balances
CashAvailable balance for buying.
HoldingsTotal oz held from Buy/Sell actions.
TotalCash + holdings × latest price.
2) Deposit / Withdraw
DepositAdds cash to your portfolio.
WithdrawRemoves cash (cannot go below zero).
3) Performance Over Time
Equity curveCalculated from your transactions and GC=F daily price history.

Troubleshooting

1) Chart or live data not loading
CauseSome sources may be blocked on certain networks/browsers.
FixRefresh the page, switch timeframe, or try again later.
2) My saved data disappeared
CauseLocal storage cleared or using a different browser/device.
FixAvoid clearing site data or use the same device/browser.
